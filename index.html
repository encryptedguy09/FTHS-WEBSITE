About Our School

Florentino Torres High School, located in Tondo, Manila, is one of Metro Manila’s oldest and most respected public high schools. Founded in 1925, FTHS has long been committed to delivering quality education while nurturing students’ academic growth, character, and community spirit. With a comprehensive curriculum—including enriched science offerings and foreign-language programs—FTHS empowers every student to pursue excellence, build values, and realize their fullest potential.

Our Programs

  • Special Program in Foreign Language (SPFL) — Japanese language, culture, and arts class for all year levels.
  • Regular Junior High School curriculum (Grades 7–10) under the standard public-school program.
  • Extracurricular / Student Organizations & Clubs — various student orgs including Scouts, Red Cross Youth, Campus Integrity Crusaders, Dancesport Club, arts & theater clubs, etc.
  • School Library / e‑Library Access — a functioning library resource for students as part of their learning support system.
